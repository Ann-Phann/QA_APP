Additional study abroad & exchange costs

  • Visa

    Study Abroad & Exchange students are required to apply for a non-award student visa depending on length of stay and purpose of the visit. Additional costs will apply.

    Please apply through the Department of Home Affairs  for a visa to study in Australia.

  • Overseas Student Health Cover (OSHC)

    Study Abroad and Exchange students are required to purchase approved Australian Overseas Student Health Cover (OSHC). The cost for OSHC is calculated based on the length of the student's program. It is a condition of a student visa to have OSHC for the entire length of your stay. 

    Exceptions

    • Students from Sweden, Norway and Belgium are covered under the reciprocal health care arrangements between their home countries and Australia.
    • Students who only study in Summer or Winter School

    It is strongly advised that Study Abroad & Exchange students have comprehensive travel insurance cover for the length of their stay in Australia. This will need to be arranged prior to departure from your home country.

  • Flights

    An economy class return flight to Adelaide varies dramatically depending on your country of origin, this must be costed in to your overall budget.

  • Books & study materials

    You should also allow at least $250 - $400AUD per semester for the cost of textbooks and other basic study materials.

    Some students will also all incur costs for specialist equipment (e.g. laboratory coats, microscopes, stethoscopes) and field trips.
